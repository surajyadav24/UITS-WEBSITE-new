Our Areas of Expertise

Information Technology/IT

IT encompasses a wide range of applications and technologies including Hardware, Software, Networks, Databases, and Cybersecurity. 

Business Process Outsourcing

The objective of IT BPO is to enable organizations to focus on their core business functions while outsourcing non-core activities to specialized service providers.  

Banking and Finance (BFSI)

Involves maintaining records or conducting Financial Transactions & helping in Risk Management, Fraud Detection, Regulatory Compliance & CRM. 

Healthcare

Includes the development & implementation of Electronic Health Records, Telemedicine, Health Information Exchange, Patient Portals, Medical Imaging etc. 

Telecom

Telecom domain encompasses various technologies, including Networking, Cloud Computing, Software-Defined Networking, Network Functions Virtualization, IoT & 5G. 

Education

Education industry uses various technology to facilitate the teaching & learning process, as well as to enhance educational outcomes.  

Media & Entertainment

Cloud computing, Big Data Analytics, AI, and Machine Learning are being used to improve the efficiency & effectiveness of various aspects of this industry. 

Public/ Govt Services

The public/government services domain uses information technology in the provision of services to citizens by public sector organizations. 

Insurance

The insurance domain relies heavily on technology to manage Customer Data, Policy Administration, Claims Processing, Underwriting, and Risk Management. 

E-Commerce

The e-commerce domain in IT refers to the use of electronic technology, specifically the internet, to facilitate the buying and selling of goods and services. 

Automotive & Industrial Engineering

IT tools such as CAD, CAM, and Simulation software are widely used in the Automotive & Industrial engineering domains to design & develop products. 

FMCG

IT solutions used in the FMCG domain include ERP software, CRM software, Supply Chain Management software, POS systems, and BI tools.
